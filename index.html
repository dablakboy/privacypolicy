Privacy Policy for Build A Pal

Last Updated: December 17, 2025

Introduction

Build A Pal ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address, username, display name (optional) when you create an account
  • Profile Data: Preferred name/nickname (optional), avatar URL (optional)
  • Pal Creation Data: Custom pal names, descriptions, personality profiles, avatar image prompts, and voice selections
  • Conversations: Messages you send to your AI pals (stored locally on device and optionally synced to cloud)
  • Marketplace Listings: If you choose to share pals in the marketplace, we collect pal details (name, description, avatar, voice, price, tags)
  • Ratings & Reviews: Optional ratings and reviews you provide for marketplace pals
  • Payment Information: Processed securely through Apple's In-App Purchase system (we don't store credit card details)

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, app version
  • Usage Analytics: App sessions, feature usage (stored locally and not transmitted to third parties without consent)
  • Performance Data: Crash logs, error reports for improving app stability (via Expo)
  • Voice Cache Data: Temporary caching of voice preview metadata to improve browsing performance

AI Service Data

  • Conversation Data: Your messages are sent to AI service provider OpenAI to generate responses based on your model selection. These providers process data according to their respective privacy policies and enterprise API terms.
  • Voice Data: Audio recordings sent to OpenAI for transcription (via gpt-4o-transcribe model through secure Supabase Edge Functions)
  • Speech Synthesis: Text sent to ElevenLabs for voice generation (via secure Supabase Edge Functions)
  • Image Generation: Text prompts sent to OpenAI DALL-E for avatar image generation

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain core app functionality (AI chat, voice synthesis, image generation)
  • Generate AI responses personalized to your pal's personality profile
  • Process in-app purchases and manage marketplace transactions
  • Calculate and distribute creator earnings for marketplace sales
  • Cache voice metadata to improve browsing performance
  • Send important notifications about your account, purchases, or app updates
  • Improve app performance, stability, and user experience
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, or misuse of the marketplace
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service

Data Sharing and Disclosure

Third-Party Services

We share data with the following service providers:

  • Supabase: Database, authentication, and cloud storage (data stored in US-based servers)
  • OpenAI: AI text generation (GPT-4o-mini), voice transcription (gpt-4o-transcribe), image generation (DALL-E)
  • ElevenLabs: Text-to-speech voice synthesis and voice library access (5000+ voices)
  • Apple: In-app purchase processing and payment handling
  • Expo: App development platform and crash reporting

Important: Your conversations are sent to AI service provide OpenAI via secure edge functions to keep API keys safe. These providers may temporarily process your data to generate responses but do not use your data to train their models (according to their enterprise API policies).

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or to:

  • Protect our legal rights
  • Prevent fraud or abuse
  • Ensure safety of users

Business Transfers

If we undergo a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the new entity.
